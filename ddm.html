Dress Designing & Making

Government College of Technology for Women Faisalabad

About Department

The Dress Designing & Making Department prepares students in fashion design, garment construction, embroidery, textile science and boutique management through practical training and creative projects.

3-Year Course Outline

First Year

  • Introduction to Fashion
  • Basic Stitching
  • Textile Science
  • Fashion Drawing
  • Fabric Identification

Second Year

  • Pattern Making
  • Garment Construction
  • Embroidery
  • CAD for Fashion
  • Fashion Illustration

Third Year

  • Advanced Fashion Design
  • Boutique Management
  • Bridal Wear
  • Industrial Training
  • Final Project
