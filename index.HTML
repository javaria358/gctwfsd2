About Us

Government College of Technology for Women Faisalabad provides quality technical education for female students. The college offers Diploma of Associate Engineering (DAE) in Computer Information Technology and Dress Designing & Making. Our mission is to prepare skilled professionals through modern teaching methods, practical training, and innovation.
